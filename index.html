THE HEART BEHIND IT

The Story Behind Silsila

Before Silsila became a space, it was an idea.

An idea that a small city deserved beautiful spaces, meaningful experiences, and a community built around creativity and connection.

What started as a dream slowly became a living, breathing place filled with conversations, coffee, and countless stories.

Today, Jagan remains deeply involved in the everyday life of Silsila—from welcoming guests and curating experiences to continuously shaping the culture that makes the space feel like home.

“If you've ever spent an afternoon at Silsila, shared a conversation, worked on a dream, or simply enjoyed a quiet coffee, you've already become a part of our story.”

— Jagan
THE FLOW

A Day At Silsila

The shifting rhythms of our space from dusk till dawn.

09:00 AM

Morning

Fresh coffee and quiet moments. The smell of fresh grinds, sunlight cutting through the glass pane, and space to breathe before the day picks up.

02:00 PM

Afternoon

Work sessions, meetings, and conversations. Co-working desks filled with ideas, soft chatter over warm plates, and new collaborations taking root.

06:00 PM

Evening

Community, connection, and slow living. Sunset music filterings, storytelling loops, and winding down with shared brews under low lighting.
