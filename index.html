Silsila is an understated living space where artisanal Kumaoni slow-fashion exists alongside carefully roasted, hand-poured specialty coffee.

Enter the Space →

A rhythm borrowed from the hills.

We believe in deliberate slowness. Silsila is not a quick stop; it is an invitation to pause, watch the mist settle over the foothill forest, and appreciate the craftsmanship behind your cup and your clothes.

Every coffee bean we source is traced directly to sustainable farms, roasted in micro-batches, and brewed with precision. Our retail collection is a curated dialogue with weavers across Uttarakhand, working exclusively with raw, vegetable-dyed fibers.

THE CONCEPT RACK

Slow textiles, handwoven.

We collaborate with regional weavers in Kumaon to design minimalist silhouettes made from natural fibers. Every piece is colored using plant-based pigments—extracted from pomegranate skin, walnut shells, and marigolds.

  • 100% Belgian linen and organic Himalayan hemp blends
  • Hand-loomed merino wraps dyed with local flora
  • Small batch productions, zero synthetic fibers
