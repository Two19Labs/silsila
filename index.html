OUR STORY

A name that means
an unbroken thread.

Silsila is the word for a chain, a continuity, the way one conversation becomes the next, one generation's recipe becomes another's. We opened our doors on Nainital Road in 2019 to give Haldwani a room for exactly that.

Our chai is brewed the slow way, our bhatt and gahat come from farms up in the Kumaon hills, and the bal mithai is made by hands that have made it for three decades. Nothing here is in a hurry, and neither should you be.

Come for a cup. Stay for the silsila.
