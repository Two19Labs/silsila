THE HEART BEHIND IT

The Story Behind Silsila

Silsila was founded by Jagan Kalakkoti to provide Haldwani with a dedicated environment for specialty coffee and curated design.

We believe a small city deserves spaces that inspire reflection. From sourcing beans to collaborating with regional weavers, everything here is selected with intent.

Jagan runs the café daily, designing the seasonal menu and curating the gallery workspace.

“We wanted to create a place that feels like an extension of home—a quiet corner where you can stay as long as you like.”

— Jagan
