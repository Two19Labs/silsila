THE MAN BEHIND THE COUNTER

Crafting Silsila, one conversation at a time.

Silsila is the manifestation of Jagan's passion for slow living and artisanal details. Stepping away from hyper-commercial spaces, Jagan created a refuge where coffee, lifestyle curations, and mountain air exist in unison. If you visit, you'll find him behind the espresso machine pouring shots or curating the fabric racks.

A Day in Jagan's Routine

06:00 AM

Mountain Mist Run

Taking a slow walk through the dewy pine forest, taking photos, and breathing in the Kumaon fresh morning breeze.

09:00 AM

Dialing-in the Espresso

Tasting and calibrating the espresso machine. Perfecting temperature and pressure parameters for the morning bean profile.

12:30 PM

Concept Store Styling

Updating the racks, checking hand-printed clothes from organic weavers, and organizing custom tags.

04:30 PM

The Slow Pour-Over Chats

Sitting down with travelers, coffee enthusiasts, and friends. Pouring a fresh batch of single-origin light roasts.

07:30 PM

Sunset Edits & Design Drops

Working on lookbook photography edits for the next fashion launch. Listening to ambient sounds and winding down.
