A quiet rhythm.

We exist in the space between the rush. Silsila is a slow refuge in Haldwani. Every cup is brewed deliberately; every thread on our racks tells a story of mountain hands.

We source clean, single-origin beans, roasted under gentle flames and brewed in local clay vessels. Our garments are curated in collaboration with small handloom hubs across Kumaon, spun with natural linen, raw wool, and vegetable dyes.
